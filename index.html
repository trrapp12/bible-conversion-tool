The Israelites originally measured length by the finger, hand, arm, span, foot, and pace. However, these are different for everyone. Eventually they had to create a common standard. This created a system that has different names and amounts for measuring lengths, making it hard for us to understand it today.

This calculator is an attempt to make the otherwise obscure measurements of the bible easily understandable by converting them into a unit more commonly understood. -- Trevor R.
